About

About (me).

I believe that great design is about crafting high-quality products that are aesthetically pleasing and easy to use.

I design digital products and ensure high-quality work that is aesthetically pleasing and easy to use. Innovating in a rapidly changing field such as software design, seeing the tangible impact of your work, and the opportunity to learn from skilled professionals and mentor interns are just a few reasons why I love my work.

Throughout my career as a design practitioner, I have crafted digital products for a variety of companies, including New Relic and Keka. My recent accomplishments include successfully leading the scaling of features in New Relic. Prior to that, I worked with a team of designers in a complete redesign of the Keka Design System, resulting in a modern platform that received high praise from users and industry experts.

With my track record of delivering outstanding digital products and leading successful design teams for more than 5 years, I am always ready to take on new challenges and push the boundaries of what is possible while nurturing and developing.
