UX Design · Keka · 2023

Rewards & Recognition

R&R directly supports employee engagement, performance, and overall organisational success.

UX Design HR Tech Keka 2023

Overview

R&R is designed to acknowledge and appreciate employee contributions to an organisation. It's an approach to motivate, engage, and retain talent by demonstrating that their efforts are valued and lead to positive outcomes.

Users

An HR R&R program is a structured way to appreciate and reward employees for achievements, efforts, or behaviours that align with company values and goals.

HR View

The HR persona owns strategic design, implementation, maintenance, and overall effectiveness of the R&R program.

Nominator View

The nominator is any employee who acknowledges a colleague's contributions, achievements, or behaviours.

Panelist View

The panelist persona reviews, evaluates, and ultimately approves or rejects nominations.

User Journeys

HR Journey

HR defines the types of recognition and reward programs, then provides training to employees, managers, and panelists on how to use the R&R system and effectively participate in programs.

Nominator Journey

The nominator acknowledges someone, selects the appropriate recognition or reward program, reviews the input, and formally submits the nomination into the system for tracking.

Panelist Journey

Panelists evaluate nominations against criteria, discuss nominations with other panelists when needed, make informed approval decisions, and confirm the reward to be granted.
